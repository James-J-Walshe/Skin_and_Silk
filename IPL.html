What is IPL Hair Removal?

IPL (Intense Pulsed Light) hair removal is a safe, non-invasive treatment designed to reduce unwanted hair growth. Using controlled pulses of broad-spectrum light, IPL targets the pigment (melanin) in hair follicles, gradually reducing their ability to regrow hair.

Benefits of IPL

  • Effective long-term hair reduction
  • Safe for various skin types
  • Less painful than traditional waxing
  • No ingrown hairs or razor burn
  • Treats large areas quickly (legs, arms, back)

What to Expect

During the session, a handheld device delivers controlled light pulses to the treatment area. Most clients describe the sensation as a mild snap on the skin, similar to a rubber band flick. Sessions are quick, and downtime is minimal—making it easy to fit into your routine.

For best results, multiple sessions are recommended, as hair grows in different cycles.

Aftercare

To achieve optimal results and keep your skin healthy, avoid direct sun exposure and tanning beds before and after treatments. Apply sunscreen regularly and follow your therapist’s aftercare advice.
