What is IPL Hair Removal?

IPL (Intense Pulsed Light) hair removal is a safe, FDA-approved treatment that uses controlled pulses of broad-spectrum light to target the pigment (melanin) in hair follicles. This advanced technology gradually reduces the hair follicle's ability to regrow, providing long-lasting hair reduction results.

Unlike traditional methods like shaving or waxing, IPL works beneath the skin's surface to address hair growth at its source. The treatment is comfortable, efficient, and suitable for most skin types, making it an ideal choice for those seeking a permanent solution to unwanted hair.

Your IPL Treatment Experience

During Your Session: Our certified technician will use a state-of-the-art handheld device to deliver precise light pulses to your treatment area. Most clients describe the sensation as a gentle snap - similar to a light rubber band flick. Each pulse treats multiple hair follicles simultaneously, making treatments both efficient and comfortable.

Treatment Duration: Sessions are surprisingly quick! Facial areas take just 10-15 minutes, while larger areas like full legs typically require 30-45 minutes. You can easily fit treatments into your busy schedule.

Results Timeline: Most clients see significant hair reduction after 6-8 sessions, spaced 4-6 weeks apart. Hair grows in different cycles, which is why multiple sessions ensure we target all follicles for optimal, lasting results.

Aftercare for Best Results

Following proper aftercare ensures optimal results and keeps your skin healthy throughout the treatment process:

  • Avoid direct sun exposure 24-48 hours before and after treatment
  • Apply broad-spectrum SPF 30+ sunscreen daily to treated areas
  • Avoid tanning beds and self-tanning products during treatment course
  • Don't pluck, wax, or thread between sessions (shaving is perfectly fine)
  • Keep treated areas moisturized with gentle, fragrance-free products
  • Avoid hot baths, saunas, or intense exercise for 24 hours post-treatment
